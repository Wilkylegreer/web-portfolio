About Me

Hi, my name is William! Most people call me Kyle and I am a computer programmer and photographer. Over the years I have found a fond love for creating things that I can think of. I've been passionate about programming from a young age, and that passion has only grown stronger over the years. My journey in programming has been marked by a deep curiosity and an insatiable desire to learn. I also recently have loved learning about the art behind photography and have started doing photoshoots of many things that are interesting to me. I hope we can work together to make your visions a reality whether in programming or photography!
